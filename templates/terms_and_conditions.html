Terms and Conditions

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Use of ReliePH

ReliePH is a community crowdfunding platform for relief and assistance efforts. By using the platform, you agree to provide accurate information, use the service responsibly, and avoid activities that may harm donors, beneficiaries, fundraisers, or the platform.

Donations

Donation amounts, payment methods, and transaction references are recorded for transparency and reporting. Donations should be made voluntarily and only after reviewing the fundraiser details.

  • Successful donations may be reflected in fundraiser totals and transaction records.
  • Pending, failed, or cancelled donations may not count toward raised funds.
  • Payment processing may depend on third-party payment providers.

Fundraisers

Fundraiser creators are responsible for submitting truthful campaign information, beneficiary details, images, and updates. ReliePH may review, restrict, or remove fundraisers that appear misleading, harmful, duplicated, or outside the platform's intended relief purpose.

Transparency Records

ReliePH may display fundraiser progress, donation references, audit logs, and blockchain-related transaction details to support public accountability. These records should not be altered, misused, or represented falsely.

Limitations

ReliePH aims to keep records accurate and the platform available, but service interruptions, payment gateway issues, and data delays can occur. Users should report concerns so the platform team can review them promptly.

Contact

For questions about these terms, contact the ReliePH team through the support channels provided by the platform.
