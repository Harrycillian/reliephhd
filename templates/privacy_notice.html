ReliePH policy

Privacy Notice

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Information We Collect

ReliePH collects information needed to operate donations, fundraisers, account access, identity verification, reports, and transparency records.

  • Account details such as name, email, phone number, and role.
  • Donation details such as amount, payment method, donor name, reference number, and status.
  • Fundraiser details such as campaign information, images, beneficiary records, and updates.
  • Technical and audit information used to protect platform integrity.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to process donations, display fundraiser progress, send receipts, manage accounts, review fundraisers, support audit trails, prevent abuse, and provide transparency features.

Public Transparency

Some fundraiser and donation information may be visible to users or administrators for accountability. Public records may include fundraiser titles, donation references, amounts, statuses, dates, and blockchain-related details. Donors may choose anonymous display when available.

Data Sharing

ReliePH may share limited information with payment processors, authorized platform staff, and related service providers when needed to complete transactions, verify records, or maintain security.

Data Protection

ReliePH uses administrative and technical safeguards to protect personal information. While no system is completely risk-free, the platform limits access to sensitive records and uses audit logs to track important changes.

Your Choices

You may update account information, choose available anonymity options for donations, and contact ReliePH for questions about your personal information or privacy concerns.
